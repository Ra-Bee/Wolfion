Privacy Policy

Effective date: 24 May 2026 · Last updated: 24 May 2026

Wolfion ("we", "us", "our") operates the Wolfion website and Android application (the "Service"). This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in, our identity provider (Clerk) processes your email address, name, and authentication tokens so we can identify you across sessions.
  • Order and business records. If you place an order or use the admin tools, we store the records you create (products, sales, inventory, costs, debts, investments, electricity bills) so you can view and manage them later.
  • Receipts and photos you upload. Images you attach to records are stored alongside those records.
  • Technical data. Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, time of request) used for security and debugging only.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and maintain the Service.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To save your business records and sync them across your devices.
  • To diagnose problems and prevent abuse.

3. How your data is stored

Authentication is handled by Clerk. Account-level admin data is stored in Google Firebase (Realtime Database) under our project. Order and product data is stored in our own Postgres database hosted on Render. Application files are served from Vercel. All providers store data on encrypted infrastructure.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service providers listed above strictly to operate the Service, and only when required to do so by law.

5. Your choices

  • You can sign out at any time from the app menu.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing us at the address below. We will action verified requests within 30 days.

6. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will always reflect the latest version.

8. Contact

Questions or deletion requests: support@wolfion.website
